Facts about France

The most visited country in the world

France ranks first in the world in terms of the number of tourists. More than 90 million people visit it annually.

At first, the Eiffel Tower was temporary

It was built for the World's Fair of 1889 and was planned to be dismantled after its conclusion. However, the building became a symbol of Paris.

Croissants are not French

Despite their popularity in France, croissants originate from Austria. Marie Antoinette brought them to France.

Cheese state

More than 1,500 types of cheese are produced in France, and each region has its own unique recipes.

Disneyland Paris

This amusement park is the most popular tourist attraction in Europe.

And it is also the most visited tourist destination in Europe, with the number of visitors exceeding 15 million people every year.
