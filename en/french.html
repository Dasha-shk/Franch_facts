Facts about the French language

Linguistic curiosities

French is the official language of 29 countries, and the language of diplomacy, culture and fashion.

Also because of its melodiousness and romantic image, French is often called the "language of love". It is rich in poetic expressions, especially related to feelings.

One of the most common languages ​​in the world

About 300 million people in 29 countries of the world speak French. It is one of the official languages ​​of such organizations as the United Nations, the European Union, NATO, and the Olympic Committee.

Language evolution

French is constantly changing. For example, the French Language Academy regularly updates the official rules, adding modern words like “selfie” or “hashtag”.
