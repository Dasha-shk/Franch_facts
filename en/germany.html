Facts about Germany

Country of castles

Germany has more than 20,000 castles, including Neuschwanstein Castle, one of the most famous in the world, which inspired the creation of the castle in Disney's fairy tales.

Homeland of cars

Germany is a leader in the production of cars. It was here that Karl Benz created the first car in 1886. Brands such as BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Audi and Volkswagen are known all over the world.

Punctuality and order

Germans are known for their punctuality. Being late is considered very impolite and planning plays an important role in life.
