Facts about the German language

Long words

German is famous for its long words. For example, Rechtsschutzversicherungsgesellschaften means “legal protection insurance companies”.

Complex grammar

The German language has four cases (nominative, genitive, dative, accusative), which require changing articles, adjectives and pronouns.

Dialects

here are many German dialects that can differ significantly from standard German (Hochdeutsch). For example, Bavarian, Swabian or Saxon dialects are often not easy to understand even for Germans themselves.
